Tender Chinese Pork Belly with Soy Sauce Eggs Recipe

Ingredients list

  • 1 pound of pork belly (in half in. cubes)
  • 4 cubes of rock sugar
  • 4 green onions (separated into green leaves and white hearts)
  • 1-2 star anise
  • 4-5 dry red peppers
  • 2-3 slices of ginger
  • 3-4 bay leaves
  • 2 tablespoons of rice wine
  • 3 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of dark soy sauce
  • Extra water for boiling
  • 4 eggs

Steps

  1. Set medium-large pot to medium-high heat.
  2. In a separate pot or kettle, start to boil 3-5 cups of water.
  3. After dicing pork into half inch cubes, place in pot (fat side down).
  4. Let pork fat melt as you wait for it to brown. Once sufficient fat is melted, turn cubes to brown all sides.
  5. Once all sides are browned, remove pork from pot and dissolve rock sugar in hot fat.
  6. Once sugar is dissolved, add pork back to pot and fry again (making sure not to burn).
  7. After a minute or so of frying pork, add in green onion hearts, star anise, red pepper, ginger, and bay leaves. Stir fry for a few minutes.
  8. Add rice wine and reduce for a few minutes.
  9. Add in soy sauce and dark soy sauce, keeping care not to burn any of the sauce.
  10. Add hot/boiling water to pot and cover ingredients.
  11. bring to a high boil, reduce heat and cover. Let simmer for 1-2 hours.
  12. While pork is simmering, add 4 eggs to a pot of cool water. Bring to a boil, cover and turn off heat. Let sit for a couple of minutes.
  13. Peel eggs and add to pot to boil remainder of time with pork.
  14. Serve over a bed of rice. Enjoy!
